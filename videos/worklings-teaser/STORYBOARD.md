## Video direction

Palette: Use the Broadside dark register as the shared world: ink-black ground, cream display, and fire-orange as the sole designed accent. Rune cyan and relic gold may appear only where they already exist inside the supplied artwork; do not introduce them as extra graphic-system accents. Typography is massive lowercase display with sparse uppercase mono chrome.

Motion: Rhythm is fast → fast → build → arena impact → heroic hold. Every frame is a directed shot with one correlated camera move across the full duration, then entrance → development → settle choreography. Idle life is limited to one hero breath and at most one energy layer. Keep the lower 17% free of load-bearing text even though captions are disabled.

Negative list: no soft pet-app language, rounded cards, drop shadows, gradient grounds, generic bokeh, fake gameplay UI, independent floating elements, slideshow enter-then-freeze motion, screensaver drift, infinite repeats, or fabricated battle footage.

Stillness allocation: Frame 4 holds for a brief pre-impact suspension immediately before the arena title lands. Frame 5 holds after the final lockup so the brand line remains readable. All other frames must develop mid-shot.
